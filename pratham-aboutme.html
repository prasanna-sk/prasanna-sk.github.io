About Me

I am T. Vijayalakshmi, an exclusive Tanjore painting artist and a student of Sri. Balaji Srinivasan. I have been in the making of Tanjore paintings for the past eight years. The paintings are custom made, done on order. I am also taking classes at my residence in Nungambakkam.

About Tanjore paintings

Tanjore paintings are unique paintings, wherein our Gods and Goddess are depicted, exhibiting a lot of intricate and meticulous work. The paintings are made using 24 Carat first quality gold foil and semiprecious stones and some American diamonds. A good painting will last for more than 40 years and can be passed on to our next generations also. They are done on water-proof plyboard, so resistant to all kinds of climatic changes. They can adorn the walls of our halls, pooja rooms, living rooms or any other place we like. It gives a lot of mental peace whenever we take a look at the paintings.
